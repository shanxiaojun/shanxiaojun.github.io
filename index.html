I am currently a junior student at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China and research intern at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof.Hanghang Tong.

My research interest lies in machine learning in general, with a special focus on dynamic graphs, continual learning, and AI4healthcare. My long-term goal is to leverage intelligent machines to discover knowledge from diverse sources, thereby leading to positive outcomes for humanity. I am open to exploring extensive topics and collaborating with others.
